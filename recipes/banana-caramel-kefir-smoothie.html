Banana Caramel Kefir Smoothie

Description

The Banana Caramel Kefir Smoothie is a creamy, indulgent beverage that seamlessly blends the natural sweetness of ripe bananas with the rich, buttery flavor of caramel. Made with probiotic-rich kefir, this smoothie not only offers a tangy, velvety texture but also provides a dose of gut-friendly benefits. A hint of vanilla and a drizzle of caramel sauce elevate the flavor profile, making it a satisfying treat that feels both nourishing and decadent.

This smoothie is a versatile option for breakfast, a midday snack, or even a dessert, offering a balanced combination of fruit, dairy, and a touch of sweetness. It’s quick and easy to prepare, requiring just a few ingredients and a blender to create a silky, flavorful drink. Top it with a sprinkle of cinnamon or a few banana slices for a bit of extra flair, and enjoy the comforting blend of caramel and banana with every sip.

Ingredients

  • 2 peeled frozen bananas, cut into chunks
  • 2 cups plain unsweetened kefir (such as Lifeway®)
  • 3 tablespoons caramel syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Add bananas, Kefir, caramel syrup, and vanilla to a blender, and process until smooth.
  2. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
